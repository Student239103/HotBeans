Frontend Developer

Job Description

We are seeking a passionate Frontend Developer to create engaging and user-friendly web interfaces. You will collaborate with designers and backend developers to bring creative concepts to life, ensuring high performance and responsiveness across all devices.

Requirements

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Vue, or Angular).
  • Understanding of responsive design principles.
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Backend Developer

Job Description

Join our team as a Backend Developer responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the server-side logic of our web applications. You will work on database management, API development, and ensuring the scalability and security of our platform.

Requirements

  • Strong experience with server-side languages (e.g., Node.js, Python, Ruby, or Java).
  • Knowledge of database technologies (SQL and/or NoSQL).
  • Experience building and consuming RESTful APIs.
  • Understanding of server architecture and cloud platforms.
  • Ability to write clean, maintainable, and testable code.

Full-Stack Developer

Job Description

We're looking for a versatile Full-Stack Developer to work on both frontend and backend aspects of our projects. You will be involved in the entire development lifecycle, from concept and design to deployment and maintenance, contributing to all layers of our applications.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Full-Stack Developer or similar role.
  • Expertise in both frontend (HTML, CSS, JS, frameworks) and backend technologies.
  • Familiarity with databases, web servers, and UI/UX design.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • A knack for visual design and utility.
